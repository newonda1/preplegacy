Why PrepLegacy

Not another place for athletic directors to enter the same information.

PrepLegacy is built to sit above the tools schools already use. MaxPreps, Hudl, and GameChanger are valuable for schedules, scores, video, current stats, and team management. PrepLegacy turns that material, plus the older history those platforms do not fully preserve, into a lasting school archive.

Current seasons

We can dig out the stats your coaches already posted.

Coaches should not have to learn another system just so the school can have a better record book. PrepLegacy can use the public data, exports, stat files, and team pages that already exist, then organize them into pages built for families, alumni, coaches, and school leaders.

Use whatever history you have

Old newspaper clippings, yearbooks, PDFs, spreadsheets, scorebooks, photos, award lists, and handwritten notes can all become part of the archive. Send the material in whatever form you have; PrepLegacy sorts it into an accessible order.

Built around the school's site

Each PrepLegacy site can be styled to match the school's current athletics presence, including colors, logos, navigation patterns, and page tone. The goal is for the archive to feel embedded inside the school experience.

Broad where national platforms are uneven

National databases are useful, but coverage and depth vary by sport. PrepLegacy can preserve the sports, eras, records, and community stories that matter to one school, even when they do not fit neatly into a national platform.

Permanent program memory

Tonight's score belongs on the tools schools already use. PrepLegacy is where the season, player, record, rivalry, and championship history remains searchable after the current year moves on.
